How does it work?



Well when gmod rejects a youtube video its not due to the video its 99% of the time youtube


So what this tool does is it takes that youtube video
downloads it as an mp4 then converts it to webm
it also downscales the video to 144p just to save storage
(Audio Quality is unaffected)
